Inter College Hackethon

India, a nation projected to drive the global production in span of 10 years, is facing many issues and computer science and IOT serves as the o to solution for many of these issues. Our hackathon looks to harness the power of amazing developers and the world of open source to develop state of the art to few problems. Our hackathon aligns with the mission and vision of Make in india and prime minister's Digital India Campaign. The hackers will be required to present a viable prototype of the solution they are building.

AMAZING TRACKS

AI/MACHINE LEARNING

Machine learning focuses on the development of computer programs that can access data and use it learn for themselves

ROBOTICS

Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing

BLOCK CHAIN

A blockchain is a decentralized, distributed, and oftentimes public, digital ledger that is used to record transactions across many computers so that any involved record cannot be altered retroactively, without the alteration of all subsequent blocks

CRYPTOGRAPHY

Cryptography involves creating written or generated codes that allow information to be kept secret. Cryptography converts data into a format that is unreadable for an unauthorized user, allowing it to be transmitted without unauthorized entities decoding it back into a readable format, thus compromising the data.

ALGORITHMS

An algorithm is a step by step method of solving a problem. It is commonly used for data processing, calculation and other related computer and mathematical operations.

OPEN HACK

Hacking is an attempt to exploit a computer system or a private network inside a computer. Simply put, it is the unauthorised access to or control over computer network security systems for some illicit purpose.

APP DEVELOPMENT

app development is the act or process by which a mobile app is developed for mobile devices, such as personal digital assistants, enterprise digital assistants or mobile phones.

AR/VR

Augmented reality (AR) is a type of virtual reality technology that blends what the user sees in their real surroundings with digital content generated by computer software.
